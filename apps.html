Whatsapp Redirect


WhatsApp Redirect is a user-friendly application designed to streamline communication on WhatsApp. With this app, you can easily open a chat with any phone number without the hassle of saving the contact to your phone. Whether you're reaching out to a new contact, a service provider, or someone you don't want to save in your address book, WhatsApp Redirect makes the process quick and efficient.

Features:

  • Instant Chat Access
  • No Contact Saving Needed
  • User-Friendly Interface
  • Fast Redirect to WhatsApp
  • Supports International Numbers
